Who I Am

I am Md. Anik Hassan, an Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering (ETE) student at Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology (RUET). My academic journey revolves around electronics, communication systems, signal processing, and embedded technologies. Through my coursework, I have developed practical experience in C/C++, Data Structures & Algorithms, MATLAB simulations, and both analog and digital communication systems.

I enjoy turning theoretical concepts into real-world engineering solutions. During my studies, I have completed multiple laboratory experiments and technical projects, including communication system implementations and signal analysis using MATLAB. One of my notable projects is an autonomous smart vacuum cleaner robot designed for floor cleaning, where I applied principles of electronics, sensors, and system design to build a functional prototype.

To me, engineering is more than studying circuits or writing code — it is about solving meaningful problems and building technology that improves everyday life. Beyond academics, I focus on continuous learning, self-improvement, and personal growth, striving to become both a better engineer and a better individual.
